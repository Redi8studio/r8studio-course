The Exercise
Pick one piece. Run it through all three skills back to back.
  • 1Open your R8 Studio Skills project in Claude.
  • 2Type clone. Upload your reference and product image. Take the prompt into Higgsfield. Generate your model shot.
  • 3Type product. Upload your reference and product image. Take the prompt into Higgsfield. Generate your product shot.
  • 4Type video. Upload your best image from steps 2 or 3. Take the prompt into Higgsfield. Generate your campaign reel.
  • 5Save all three. That is your full content set for one piece.
Do this for every piece in your collection. That is your launch.
