How the Clone Skill Works
The Clone skill is the foundation of the entire system. It lets you place any jewellery piece onto a model image using two inputs and Claude as your prompt engine.

You upload two images. Image 1 is your reference. The model, the pose, the lighting, the setting, the mood you want. Image 2 is your product. The actual jewellery piece from your supplier site or product page. Type the word clone. Claude reads both images, extracts everything from the reference, describes your piece accurately, and outputs a complete Higgsfield prompt. You copy it and paste it straight in.
Reference Image Rules
Lighting must be clear
The output light will match your reference light. Soft, hard, directional, diffused. Whatever is in the reference comes through in the output.
Jewellery must match the form
Find a reference with a model wearing the same type of jewellery you want to generate. Ring reference for a ring. Earring reference for an earring.
Model and pose must be clear
The closer the reference is to what you want the output to look like the better. Blurry or unclear references produce blurry or unclear outputs.
Avoid heavily filtered images
Heavily edited or filtered references confuse the output. Find references that feel genuinely photographic.
