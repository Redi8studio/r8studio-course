How to Access Them
They do not have an open online store. You access their catalogue by contacting them directly via email first. Once you have communicated with them they will set you up with a trade account.
  • 1Email Vesmer to introduce yourself and your brand
  • 2Mention that you were referred by R8 Studio. This gives you first priority and unlocks future discounts on your orders
  • 3Tell them you are looking to place wholesale orders with a minimum order quantity (MOQ). You want to start small, test the market, and scale your orders once you know what sells
  • 4Once they respond they will guide you through creating your account
  • 5You will then have access to their full catalogue and pricing
What to Say on First Contact
Keep it short. Keep it professional.

Tell them who you are, what you sell, and that you are looking to source 18k gold and silver pieces for your collection. Always mention R8 Studio by name in your first message. This ensures you get first priority and access to member discounts on future orders. They are based in Spain so if you speak Spanish that helps. English works fine too.
