What This Is
Once you have your content ready and your stock sourced, you need to get your orders to your customers. Reliably. Internationally. Without spending a fortune on shipping.

This is the logistics partner R8 Studio personally uses and recommends.
Asendia
Asendia is an international shipping and logistics company established as a joint venture between La Poste (France) and Swiss Post (Switzerland). They deliver to over 200 destinations worldwide and specialise in cross-border e-commerce shipping for independent brands and retailers. Fully tracked. Carbon neutral. Built for small businesses shipping internationally.
A Personal Recommendation

R8 Studio has personally used Asendia Netherlands and Asendia Spain and had a strong experience with both. These are the two offices listed here as a starting point. However Asendia operates in over 17 countries. If you are based outside of the Netherlands or Spain, search for your local Asendia office at asendia.com — there is a good chance they operate in your country too.

Why Asendia for Jewellery
Jewellery is small. Jewellery is high value. And jewellery needs to arrive looking exactly as it left. Asendia handles small parcels with full tracking, customs clearance, and delivery confirmation across over 200 destinations.
How to Get Started
  • 1Check if Asendia operates in your country at asendia.com
  • 2If you are based in or shipping from Europe, contact the Netherlands or Spain office directly using the email above. This connects you straight to the sales representative
  • 3Tell them you are a jewellery brand owner looking to set up an account for international e-commerce shipping
  • 4Mention you were referred by R8 Studio
  • 5They will walk you through account setup, pricing, and the right service tier for your volume
What to Say on First Contact
Keep it short. Keep it professional.

Tell them who you are, what you sell, roughly how many orders you expect to ship per month, and the main countries you are shipping to. Mention R8 Studio by name. Then let them come back to you with the right solution.
